Introduction

The international doctoral program in Mathematics at Tbilisi State University was established in 2017 to facilitate the education of doctoral students at a high level in cooperation with international partners. Students in the program have a coadvisor at a partner university and spend one semester abroad. They may be supported by fellowships. Topics featured in the program include partial differential equations, mathematical analysis, algebra and topology, numerical analysis, and stochastics. The program is supported by the LINKVolkswagen Foundation and run in partnership with colleagues at the University of Göttingen.
